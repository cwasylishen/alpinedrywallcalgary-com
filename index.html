What we build

Steel stud, drywall, and ceilings, top to bottom.

Two trades, executed by crews who run them every day on commercial sites across Calgary and southern Alberta.

Steel stud framing & drywall

Complete interior framing and drywall packages for commercial, institutional, and tenant improvement projects. Clean lines, square corners, finishes ready for paint.

  • Metal stud and track framing systems
  • Fire-rated and acoustic-rated assemblies
  • Moisture-resistant and abuse-resistant board
  • Level 4 and Level 5 finishes

Acoustical ceilings

Suspended T-bar grids, drywall ceilings, and specialty acoustical work. We coordinate with mechanical, electrical, and lighting trades so the ceiling drops in clean the first time.

  • T-bar grid systems from every major supplier
  • Acoustical and decorative tile selection
  • Custom suspension heights and bulkheads
  • Integration with HVAC, sprinkler, and lighting
